Welcome!

You, a brave warrior of the Mountain Leauge, have decided to explore the forgotten caves of Oblitus Tenebris, once the most majestetic mountain in the world before a mysterious cause destroyed it. A rumor was spread that there is a jewel of mass energy inside the remains of the mountain that makes its wearer invincible, and your objective is to find it and take it for yourself.

The fallen mountain is not empty, though. Legend has it that the mountain crawls with creatures of the dark, protecting the jewel. No one knows why. You have to get across the obstacles you meet by making the right choices.

How to play

You will get a short summary of the current plot and what is happening. After that, you will get two options of what to do. Each of the choices will give you an outcome, one of which is the right choice and will continue your journey, while the other will lead to your death.
